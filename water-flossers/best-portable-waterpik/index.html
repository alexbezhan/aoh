Are you looking for a portable Waterpik? There are a lot of them and it may be hard to decide what to buy. So we compared the best models that we think can be nominated as the best portable Waterpik. So here is what we got. (people sometimes call it water pick and we understand that. It’s easy to make this mistake, but the right name is waterpik or oral irrigator.)

Our considerations

Power source

A portable Waterpik can use one of two power sources:

  • wall outlet
  • AA batteries

It’s important to understand and take into consideration. AA batteries are more convenient, so why not choose a product that is powered by AA batteries?

And the answer to this question lays deep in the power capacity of AA batteries. They can’t generate enough power for the product to clean your teeth and gums. That’s why products that work from them have weak performance.

So the clean winner here is wall outlet. Most of the products have convenient inductive chargers that are easy to use.

Waterpik Cordless Advanced

Inductive charger provides 4 hours rapid charge when most of the alternatives take up to 8 hours. It’s a clear winner on the charging field.

Panasonic EW1211A

The charging base plugs into the wall outlet. It has a wall mount for better convenience. You can keep the second nozzle on the base as well, which is handy.

Jetpik JP200

The charger is ultra small and portable. It uses USB port, so you can use your Laptop to charge it. It’s also good for travel since you can use it worldwide.

Waterpik Cordless Freedom

This one works from 3 AA batteries. And this is why it has low performance. It should work for you if you have very sensitive gums, though.

Performance

High performance is essential for a good cleaning of your mouth. Some products make it by generating high pressure, others combine additional features that provide high performance in the end product.

Waterpik Cordless Advanced

High performance is what makes this product stand out from a lot of alternatives. It has high pressure. So it cleans well. No questions here.

Panasonic EW1211A

This one has high performance. It works well for braces as well. If you use it on a regular basis you will see the results. And you will love it.

Jetpik JP200

The pressure level on JetPik is lower, but the overall performance is very high. And pulsating dental floss is what makes work the whole equation. No alternatives have this feature. At the end, the product is quiet but very performant. The downside is that you have to replace the floss cartridges on a regular basis. Usually, it’s 1-2 times a month.

Waterpik Cordless Freedom

This one has less power than others. And that’s why the performance is weak. If you have sensitive gums then it may work for you. Otherwise, it’s not enough for a decent cleaning.

Longevity

The better the product is made the longer it will work. So it’s an important feature to take into consideration. In general, the more experience the company has the better products it makes and the longer warranty it provides.

Waterpik Cordless Advanced

It’s a premium quality product. Waterpik made it by fixing all the flaws in its previous models. So it is high quality. It has 2-Year Warranty. And it will work longer in most cases. This is the most robust product when compared to alternatives. No questions.

Panasonic EW1211A

It’s made of quality plastic. Panasonic cares about the longevity of their products and you can feel that.

Jetpik JP200

JetPik had some issues with battery life. People reported that it died after few months of work. Since then the company produced Revision 2 and the new model has no issues with quality.

Waterpik Cordless Freedom

This one is not as good as others. It’s made of cheap plastic. And when you hold it you can feel that. It’s cheap, so for the money it’s okay. But it has some quality concerns, especially when compared to other water flossers.

Convenience

People use water flossers on a daily basis. So convenience is a must have. Without it, you will not use the product every day and will not have the results you want. Compact size and portability also contribute to the convenience level.

Waterpik Cordless Advanced

Quiet work, comfortable grip with chrome accents and ease of use. This product earns a high score on the convenience axes. You will love it.

Panasonic EW1211A

Rubberized grip and comfortable grip. Panasonic has high convenience. You can feel that the company cares about you.

Jetpik JP200

JetPik stands out from others. JetPik cup serves as a water tank and the handle is small like a Toothbrush. So it’s very easy to use. The convenience is good no doubt. One thing to remember is that you have to connect the rubber tube from handle to the cup before use.

Waterpik Cordless Freedom

The unit is bulky, but it’s okay to use. One thing to note is that you will get a weird feeling. When the water tank is full it is heavy. And when it empties to the end it becomes lightweight. So you have to get used to this weird feeling.

Water tank capacity

A portable water flosser has to have a big enough water tank for convenient use. But small enough for portability. So it has to be something in the middle.

Waterpik Cordless Advanced

The water tank is 7 oz (210 ml). It’s right in the sweet spot. It is big enough for convenience use and small enough for portability. It’s a clear winner.

Panasonic EW1211A

The water tank is 4.4 oz (130 ml). You will have to refill it for a decent cleaning.

JetPik JP200

The capacity of JetPik cup is 300 10 oz (350 ml). So it’s a biggest which is cool. Another thing is that you can use any cup/bottle you have. You just connect the tubing from the handle to the bottle and you are good to go. So this flexibility makes JetPik a really good choice.

Waterpik Cordless Freedom

5 oz water tank is good. It’s better than Panasonic obviously. It’s still not enough for a decent cleaning, though. So you will have to refill it few times.

The winner is Waterpik Cordless Advanced

Waterpik Cordless Advanced is the clear winner. It is high quality and provides great performance. It’s really convenient and ease to use and by far the best portable Waterpik product of all times.

The second place goes JetPik

JetPik is a high-performance product. The variety of different accessories you get is impressive. It is really a well-thought product. Easy to use and well worth its money.
