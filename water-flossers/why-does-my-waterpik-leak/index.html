Why does a Waterpik leak

The reason of the leakage that I see the most frequently is that users leave water in the water reservoir. In this case, water leaks through the bottom of the water tank. Most of the Waterpik models do have a valve in the bottom of the water reservoir, but it is supposed to hold the water only for a short period(while you are water flossing.) And when you leave some water in the water reservoir for the whole night night, the valve can’t hold the water and this causes water tank leakage. So to fix the leaking water tank please make sure you empty the water reservoir and no liquid is left in it.

In case you see that your water tank is leaking immediately once you fill it with the water then the reason may be the bad water valve. In this case, I recommend hitting official customer support at waterpik.com.

Another reason of leaking may be the handle popped out of the handle holder. In this case, the handle is leaking the water that is left in the cord(hose). The very first advise I can give here is to put the handle in the right position in the holder so the tip is directed upwards.

If there are some issues or it’s hard to put the handle in the right position(for some reason) I recommend sucking the water that is left in the hose with your mouth so there is no water left in the hose. But generally I recommend putting the hose in the holder and you will not have any issues with it.

There last possible cause of the leakage in your Waterpik may be the bad hose. In this case, I recommend hitting customer support on waterpik.com or on Twitter. They will send you the replacement hose for your Waterpik. Their customer support usually answers in 1-2 days(when I contacted them they replied pretty quickly.)
