Waterpik Ultra Water Flosser for braces

Waterpik Ultra comes with several different tips, and one of them is a special Orthodontic Tip that makes it perfectly suitable for braces.

This tip has a little brush on the end that cleans my teeth well. It also pulsates water, and I add my mouthwash to the water reservoir to make it more efficient. I usually use it after every meal. The water pressure quickly removes any food from my braces and in between teeth. It is also fun to see how much food is left on my teeth after brushing.

Ultra Water Flosser is loud, though. But it is so effective and easy to use that I’m okay with that. If you have braces, I absolutely recommend getting Waterpik. You will see how it makes your life easier.
