What is the best Waterpik for travel: Making the right choice

What to look for in a Waterpik for travel?

Travel Waterpik Water Flosser should be compact and portable. The portability should not come with an expense of bad cleaning though and it can be hard to decide what Waterpik to choose for travel.
There is no one size fits all though and I have to ask you few questions in order to pick the right Waterpik for you.

Question #1. Do you travel inside US or overseas?

If you travel within US then you don’t need a universal Voltage and your choice will be easier. You can jump right into traveling inside US section.

If you plan to travel overseas or maybe to just want your Waterpik to work everywhere in the world then your water flosser should have a universal Voltage that is compatible with any network standard in the world. Feel free to continue reading about best Waterpik for travelling overseas.

What is the best Waterpik for travel within US?

Ok, so you plan to travel only in the US and it’s fine. Now you have to answer the last question:
Question #2. Do you want a cordless Waterpik or you don’t mind having a cord charger?

If you are into cordless models then the best Waterpik is Waterpik Cordless Freedom WF-30. It’s the most compact Waterpik water flosser you can find on the market.

It’s main features are:

  • Works from 3 AA batteries. It makes this water flosser the most portable one out of any Waterpik water flossers available on the market.
  • 2 different pressure settings allow you adjust pressure to your needs.
  • 5 Ounces (150 ML) water reservoir is not a lot, but when you want a portable and compact water flosser you can’t have a huge water reservoir. Though it provides 30+ seconds of work, which also means you will have to refill it to finish cleaning your mouth.
  • 3 tips included. Two Classic Jet Tips are good for general use and one Orthodontic Tip is the best for braces.
  • Microfiber Travel Bag included that makes carrying your Waterpik more convenient and prevents leakage.
  • It provides the maximum water pressure of 75 PSI which makes a good teeth cleaning

To read the full review on Waterpik Cordless Freedom WF-30 click here.

If you don’t mind to carry a cord then you are lucky because there is a good Waterpik model that is powerful and yet portable and will make wonders to your mouth. It is a Waterpik WP-65W Water Flosser and it’s a lot more powerful than it’s cordless brother. While it is larger than cordless model, it has a water reservoir that you can invert and cover the whole water flosser with it and it becomes very compact.

Waterpik WP-60 has the following features:

  • Works from 110v Voltage with a socket plugin
  • 6 different pressure settings, so you can really tinker the right pressure of your preference
  • 35 Ounces (1000 ML) water reservoir, which is the biggest water reservoir in the whole Waterpik Water Flossers line
  • It comes with 3 tips included
  • It provides the maximum water pressure of 90 PSI which is a lot and more than enough to clean your mouth and even if you got braces

So with this guy you get a fully-fledged water flosser, though it takes more space and requires a cord plugin.

What is the best Waterpik for travel overseas?

The same as with travel within US you have to answer the second and the last question:

Question #2. Do you want a maximum portability and smallest size without any cords involved or you don’t mind having a cord charger?

If you are want a Waterpik without any cords then the best Waterpik choice for you would be Waterpik Freedom WF-03 that I discussed earlier. Please click here to read about it.

If you don’t mind putting a plugin into a wall socket before using your Waterpik then you are really lucky because Waterpik Traveler WP-300 is right for you and it’s one of the most powerful yet compact models on the market. It has the following features:

  • It has a Universal Voltage, which means you can plug it in at anywhere in the world and it will work.
  • It has 3 pressure settings, though they are made in a form of dial so you can really place the dial in any position and get the pressure level you really prefer.
  • The water reservoir is 15 Ounces (450 ML) and provides 60+ seconds of work which is more than enough for a decent cleaning.
  • It comes with 4 tips (including Orthodontic Tip, that is great for braces.)
  • The maximum water pressure is 90 PSI that is one of the best (and subjectively it’s even better than some other models with 100 PSI stated in their specifications, such as Aquarius.)
